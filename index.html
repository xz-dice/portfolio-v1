    Xandice Armah is an open-format DJ from London and co-founder of Gal Pals, an LGBTQ+ dance party centring women and excellent pop music. Xandice also DJs as part of the Brownton Abbey collective under the moniker Xzan. Xzan began their career playing at dingy basement birthday parties and are the biggest proponent for playing what you love and playing it loud. This mantra comes to life in the form of their high energy, hyperactive sets, ricocheting between queer pop party anthems and deep-cut club trax and wildcards.They are currently Mykki Blanco's UK tour DJ and have previously played at Glastonbury, Primavera and Brighton Festival. Xandice is a transmasculine non-binary person who uses they/them pronouns and in their spare time they enjoy playing video games, worrying about whether their plants have had enough water, and eating spicy noodles. They still find themselves occasionally DJing in basements, albeit much larger ones.
