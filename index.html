    Xandice Armah is a full-stack web developer in training, an open-format DJ from London and
    co-founder of Gal Pals.


    Which is an LGBTQ+ dance party centring women and excellent pop music.

    Xandice began their career playing at dingy basement birthday parties and are the biggest proponent for playing what you love and playing it loud. This mantra comes to life in the form of their high energy, hyperactive sets, ricocheting between queer pop party anthems and deep-cut club trax and wildcards.They are currently Mykki Blanco's UK tour DJ and have previously played at Glastonbury, Primavera Sound, and Brighton Festival.
